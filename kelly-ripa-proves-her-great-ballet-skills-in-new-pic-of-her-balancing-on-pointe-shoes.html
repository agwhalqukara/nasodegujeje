Kelly Ripa Proves Her Great Ballet Skills In New Pic Of Her Balancing On Pointe Shoes

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Kelly Ripa has so many hidden talents! The TV personality proved she is even good at ballet when she shared a pic in which she was shown gracefully standing on pointe shoes!

Earlier today, Ripa took to her IG account to share the great photo with her many followers.

She can be seen wearing a grey tank top and a pair of sweatpants and balancing perfectly on pointe shoes for the camera.

Her hair was gathered away from her face and into a half-up style.

As she was seemingly studying her form in a mirror, Kelly kept her arms out by her sides.

In the caption, she wrote: ‘Practice makes……um…….something? Join #kellyandryan #guinessworldrecord attempt-most #balletdancers #enpointe simultaneously.’

https://www.instagram.com/p/B1HsiJbDEMA/

Since she hashtagged Ryan Seacrest as well, it sounds like the man was working hard in the dance room practicing ballet as well!

As fans may know, Live with Kelly and Ryan is attempting to break a Guinness World Record – Most Ballet Dancers En Pointe Simultaneously – so the practicing is definitely not for nothing.

Their attempt to break the record is set to take place on September 10 alongside American Ballet Theatre principal dancers Isabella Boylston and James Whiteside also joining in.

To break this record, the group needs to amount to at least 245 people balancing on ballet shoes for a whole minute.

In a joint statement released via Pointe Magazine, Boylston and Whiteside stated that: ‘This record breaking challenge’s a great way to share our art form with a larger audience and to make everyone feel included. All are welcome to participate and everything you have to do is just stand en pointe!’

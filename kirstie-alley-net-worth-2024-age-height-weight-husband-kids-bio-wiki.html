Early Life

Kirstie Alley was born on 12th January in 1951. She was born in Kansas in the United States of America. Her father was Robert Deal Alley. He owned a lumber company. Her mother, Lillian, was a housewife. She has a brother Craig and a sister Colette. She went to Wichita Southeast High School. She graduated from high school in 1969. She attended Kansas State University in 1969.

After moving to Los Angeles, she worked as an interior designer. She participated in the famous game show Match Game in the year 1979. In the first round, she won $500, and in the second round, she won $5500. She appeared on another popular game show called password Plus in 1980. The next year in 1981, unfortunately, her mother, Lillian, died in a car accident.

Personal Life

She married her high school sweetheart in 1970. Her husband’s name was Bob Alley, and surprisingly her father’s name was also Bob Alley. After seven years, the couple got divorced.

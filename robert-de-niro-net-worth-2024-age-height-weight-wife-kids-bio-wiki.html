Early Life

Robert Anthony De Niro Jr. was born in Manhattan, New York, on August 17, 1943, to his parents Robert De Niro Sr. and Virginia Admiral. He grew up in Manhattan.

He has attended several schools like Rhodes Preparatory School and High School of Music and Art but did not complete his graduation. He made his stage debut at the age of 10.

Personal Life

In 1976, Robert De Niro got married to Diahnne Abbott. They have a son named Raphael. The couple divorced in 1988. From 1997 to 1999, he was married to Grace Hightower. The couple has a son named Elliot. He has also got three other children named Aaron Kendrick, Julian Henry, and Helen Grace, born through surrogacy.

In 2016, De Niro criticized Donald Trump. He was secretly baptized as a Catholic as his mother was an atheist. In 2006, he was granted Italian citizenship.

Samantha Markle Slams Half-Sister Meghan Markle And Defends Their Dad Thomas Once Again!

Publish date: 2024-09-03

Will all of this drama in the Markle family ever end? Meghan’s older half-sister Samantha slammed the Duchess and defended their father Thomas Markle once again!

As you may know, the man stopped by Good Morning Britain and talked about getting ghosted by his Royal daughter, and now, Samantha decided to criticize her sister and defend their dad on Twitter.

The Times of London shared a quote from the show on Twitter, in which Thomas told Meghan he loves her very much and wants to make amends before the holidays.

When one user commented that he ‘Loves her so much he keeps getting his paydays with the media,’ Samantha was quick to fire back.

‘He wasn’t paid more on if you read or listen to the interview,’ she explained.

Indeed, Piers Morgan tweeted that the man had ‘received no payment’ for this interview.

Another follower was less aggressive, saying they feel for Thomas but still suggested he should have reached out to Meghan in private and stopped making their estrangement a public affair.

‘He did reach out privately to exhaust, but this woman thinks it’s cool to ghost an entire family, best friend of 30 years, ex-husband who treated her very well, and trade everyone out for the Royals and Clooneys,’ Samantha replied.

Meanwhile, one user also slammed Meghan, claiming she should stop acting like a child and that it was her relationship with Prince Harry that brought her father into the spotlight in the first place.

As you can imagine, Samantha totally agreed, and she added that she should ‘start behaving like a humanitarian with a good and compassionate heart.’

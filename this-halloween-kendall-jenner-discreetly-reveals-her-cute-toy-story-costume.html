Publish date: 2024-09-12

On October 29th Kendall Jenner posted an Instagram shot of herself as Jessie the redheaded cowgirl from Toy Story for Halloween 2022. The Kardashians star who just dyed her brown hair red wore a crop top chaps with denim shorts and a cowboy hat giving the character’s attire a s*xual adult twist.

The caption the model used was an excerpt from a sentence spoken by Jessie in the 1999 Disney-Pixar animated film Toy Story 2 in which Jessie made her first appearance.

Now spanning four films the successful franchise follows a bunch of toys that belonged to a young boy named Andy and after he goes off to college a young girl named Bonnie.

Welcome to the new look CelebMix!

Publish date: 2024-09-06

Hello and welcome to a new look CelebMix! After a whole year of those pesky coloured balls, we’ve decided to try out something new and the result is right here!

Read on for more information on the new-look CelebMix!

A whole new look

Our new look is colourful, vibrant and puts content at the focus. Now you can see the hottest posts of the day right at the top of the website, giving you even more from the CelebMix you love.

A mobile-first design

Our new design has been tailored specifically to the mobile platform.

No more difficult clicks… CelebMix is now mobile-first so you can enjoy us on the go

Room to grow

Our new look has only just gone live, but we’re determined to make it even better. We’ll be asking for feedback over the next few weeks and making changes based on what you have to say.

We hope you like what we’ve done. Let us know your thoughts on Twitter using @CelebMix.

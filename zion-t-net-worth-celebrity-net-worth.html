What is Zion.T's Net Worth?

Zion.T is a South Korean hip hop artist and record producer who has a net worth of $8 million. Zion.T was born in South Korea in April 1989. His career started off in 2011 when he collaborated with other Korean hip hop artists. Zion.T released his debut studio album Red Light in April 2013. The album reached #4 on the Korean charts.

He also released the extended play in December 2013. Zion.T has collaborated with artist including G-Dragon, Infinite H, SHINee, and Kim Jonghyun. His single "No Make Up" was released in October 2015. In 2014 Zion.T won Best R&B and Soul album at the Korean Music Awards.
